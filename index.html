Things to Do

Your days here

From morning hikes to evening bonfires, every day feels full.

🐎

Visit the Farm

Horses, donkeys, mules, sheep, goats, and chickens. Walk over and feed the animals — bring carrots! Half a mile from most homes.

🪷

Pick Blueberries

In July, the fields around the homes are covered in wild blueberry bushes. Pick to your heart's content and bring them home for pies.

🚶

Hiking Trails

300 acres of trails on the property, plus world-class hiking in the Shawangunks, Neversink Gorge with 3 waterfalls, and Sam's Point ice caves.

🔥

Bonfires & S'mores

Every home has a fire pit. Roast marshmallows, tell stories, and watch the stars — no light pollution up here.

🏓

Pool & Pickleball

Community pool one mile away (seasonal). Brand new free pickleball and basketball courts in town.

🎢

Family Attractions

Legoland (45 min), Kartrite Indoor Water Park (30 min), Resorts World Casino (30 min), Bethel Woods concerts, boat rentals on mountain lakes.

🍴

Local Dining & Nightlife

Mountaindale has live music, a creek-side bar, and great pizza. Woodburn has kosher supermarkets. Ellenville and Monticello have dozens of restaurants.

🕍

Nearby Synagogues

Walking distance to Orthodox synagogues in summer. Year-round synagogue in town (2 miles). Kosher supermarkets in Woodburn, less than 5 miles.
